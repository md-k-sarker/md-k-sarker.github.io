I am an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at the Bowie State University. I love to invent new stuff (research), share knowledge (teaching), and at the same time build stuff (software engineering). My research domain spans the broad field of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and its application. Some specific topics of focus include but are not limited to Explainable Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning, Knowledge Graph, Semantic Web, and CyberSecurity.
